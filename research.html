Current Research

My research focuses on the intersection of human visual perception and deep learning algorithms. Below are some of my ongoing projects.

Human Visual System and Peripheral Perception

Investigating how the human eye processes information in the far periphery and its implications for artificial vision systems.

  • Joint study with the University of Auckland.
  • Psychophysics consultation from the University of Lancaster.
  • Integration of Vision Mātauranga perspectives.

Deep Learning for Computer Vision

Applying convolutional neural networks (CNNs) to model aspects of human vision and improve AI-based image recognition.

  • Using real-world eye-tracking data.
  • Optimizing CNNs for visual field mapping.
